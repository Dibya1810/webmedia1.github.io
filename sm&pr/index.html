MESSAGE FROM THE HOD'S DESK

DR.GOKUL CHADRA MONDAL,HOD,DEPARMTENT OF CONSTRUCTION ENGINEERING

My dear students,
Let me grab this chance as an opportunity to thank you for your innovative initiatives, first of its kind in the university for information networking regarding Construction Engineering Dept The Dept, since its inception in 1989 has been rendering services specially consultancy testing to both govt and non govt sectors including major players in the field The role of the Dept in the Industry Institute Partnership programme of the University reflects the true blend of theory practice in solving real life problems
The need of the hour is to professionally focus the Dept to the construction industries by aligning the bonding between the alumni and the present students to portray the roles our students can play making a win win game for both the students and the construction industry
I do feel believe that this effort will help achieve the mission through this innovative vision
Thanking you,
